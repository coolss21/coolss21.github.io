African Wildlife

The epic savanna hosts one of Earth's greatest wildlife gatherings. Herds containing millions of wildebeest, zebra and antelope migrate following seasonal rains, ever wary of prides of lions, leopards stealthily lurking through scrubs, and hyenas cackling. Elephants in great numbers lumber across plains, kicking up red dust clouds alongside warrior-like giraffes. Colourful birds like lilac-breasted rollers streak bright hues over acacia trees dotting the landscape. The diversity astounds even in harsh deserts with meerkat sentries posted over families. Truly a kingdom for majestic beasts.
