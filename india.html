Indian Wildlife

Bengal tigers prowl the lush jungles as majestic peacocks fan their feathers to dance. Asian elephants lumber through forests foraging alongside mischievous monkeys that scamper up canopy trees filled with squawking parakeets. Camels traverse the Thar desert passing camouflaged chameleons and hissing king cobras hidden amongst the dunes. The bustling cities still host wandering cows considered sacred, stray dogs opportunistically scavenging, and even leopards stealthily sneaking about. India's contrasting landscapes foster wonderfully diverse wildlife.
